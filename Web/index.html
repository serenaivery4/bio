ABOUT

Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer, Abel Tesfaye, better knwon as The Weeknd, came into the music industry in 2011 with the release of his mixtape House Of Balloons. Since he has released five studio albums that have collaborations with other hit artist such as Drake, Kendrick Lamar, and Draft Punk. In 2012, The Weeknd partnered with Amir "Cash" Esmailian to bulid the record label XO. XO currently has five Canadian artists signed including The Weeknd. The Weeknd and his record label have gained global recognition for their music. Recently, The Weeknd sold out his first stadium concert in Seoul, Korea’s Gocheck Sky Dome. He described the event as “a beautiful sea of people” he also put “#stadiumboy”.


Albums

The Weeknd has a total of four studio albums and one Ep. His music can be found of Spotify or anywhere else music is streamed.
